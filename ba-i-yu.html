ba i yu

we made the game easier or harder, depending on who you ask

Ba I Yu is a community-made levelpack managed by Three Layer Studios and develpoed mainly by SilverRush and amateurprocoal. The levelpack consists of shrunken maingame levels, as well as some BONUS levels!

Download v0.12.0 here

To add into your baba, unzip and REPLACE it with the existing ba i yu folder (if it exists) in (Baba)/Data/Worlds.
