Ba I Yu - /bɑ ɑI jU/ or /bɑ ɪ jU/, but NEVER /bɑ i jU/

/I/ means /ij/ or /ɪj/ or /i/ or /ɪ/, and /U/ means /ʉː/ or /ʉw/

We made the game easier or harder, depending on who you ask

Ba I Yu is a community-made levelpack managed by Three Layer Studios and developed mainly by SilverRush and amateurprocoal, with a HUGE chunk of levels from 13520. The levelpack consists of shrunken maingame levels, as well as some BONUS levels! Recently I have "re"joined the "main" development team, so expect more frequent updates from me!

This project is now inactive. It may be revived in the future, who knows?
