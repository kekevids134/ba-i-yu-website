Ba I Yu is a community-made levelpack managed by Three Layer Studios and developed mainly by SilverRush and amateurprocoal. The levelpack consists of shrunken maingame levels, as well as some BONUS levels! Recently I have "re"joined the "main" development team, so expect more frequent updates from me!
